aspcol.filterclasses

Implementations of various types of filters

Implementations for standard linear convolution can be found in scipy.signal and aspcore.

  • Weighted overlap-add (WOLA) [crochiereWeighted1980, ruizComparison2021]

  • IIR filter

  • Mean with forgetting factor
